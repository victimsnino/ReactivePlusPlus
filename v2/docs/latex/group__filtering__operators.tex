auto rpp\+::operators\+::throttle (\begin{DoxyParamCaption}\item[{rpp\+::schedulers\+::duration}]{period }\end{DoxyParamCaption})}



Emit emission from an Observable and then ignore subsequent values during {\ttfamily duration} of time. 



Actually this operator just keeps time of last emission, skips values ​​until duration has passed, then forwards value, updates timepoint and etc...

\begin{DoxyParagraph}{Performance notes\+:}

\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item No any heap allocations at all
\item No any copies/moves of emissions, just passing by const\& to predicate and then forwarding
\item Obtaining "{}now"{} every emission
\end{DoxyItemize}
\end{DoxyParagraph}
